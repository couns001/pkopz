about the designer

Born in Myanmar/Burma, Jeera (Kaung Htet Thar) has always had an eye for inventive, expressive design. His work actualises elements of high fashion in a playfully subversive way.

Jeera's work is a testament to the wide range of experiences, including his time living in Japan. Like his impressive range of spoken languages, Jeera brings a myriad of different design languages to bear on this conversation with the contemporary world.

Unafraid of hard work and skill development, PKOPZ works with a diverse range of textile manufacturers, stylists and artists to bring progressive, daring fashion to everyday consumers.
