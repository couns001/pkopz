about the designer

Born in Myanmar/Burma, Jeera (Kaung Htet Thar) has always had an eye for inventive, expressive design. His work, PKOPZ actualises elements of high fashion in a playfully subversive way.

PKOPZ is a testament to the designer's wide range of experiences in Burma, Japan, Aotearoa and Australia. Like the designer's impressive range of spoken languages, PKOPZ brings a myriad of different design languages to bear on this conversation with the contemporary world.

PKOPZ draws on and partners with a diverse range of textile manufacturers, stylists and artists to bring progressive, daring fashion to everyday consumers.
